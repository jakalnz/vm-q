VM Symptom & Trigger QuestionnaireVestibular migraine self-report tool
What this is: a self-report tool to help you and your clinician understand the pattern, triggers, and impact of your vestibular migraine (VM) symptoms in more detail. It is educational only and does not provide a diagnosis. Your responses stay in this browser tab unless you choose to download or share them — nothing is sent anywhere automatically.
Phase headers — tap to set how long that phase typically lasts for you.  |  Symptom cells — click to cycle severity (0→5). Right-click (or long-press) to set how often this symptom occurs in that phase. Hover for descriptors.
